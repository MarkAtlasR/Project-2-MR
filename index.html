SEPECAT JAGUAR T.2/T.4 FROM AIRFIX

AS SOON as Airfix’s 1/48 SEPECAT Jaguar GR.1/GR.1A was seen to have a separate nose section (and the inclusion of a second pilot figure), there was huge anticipation regarding a two-seat variant. The company has duly obliged with a SEPECAT Jaguar T.2 & T.4 (A11011), which features three markings schemes. On opening the sturdy box you’ll find 13 styrene runners, ten of which are common with the initial GR.1/GR.1A boxing (see AMW171, February 2025 for a full build). Three new frames provide the four-piece cockpit glazing and extra head-up display combiner panels, plus the two-seat nose, an extra ejection seat (with a choice of early/late styles) and new cockpit tub with separate side consoles. In addition, several underwing items from the initial kit are employed here, such as the blanking plate on the right-hand cannon fairing (the T.2/T.4 has just a single 30mm Aden cannon, on the left-hand side) and the GPS antenna ‘bump’ on the fuselage spine for the T.4 airframes.

The larger cockpit area is arguably even more impressive than the single-seater, thanks to the capacious glazing, allowing the modeller to pose it either open or closed. In addition, there’s a choice between early-style instrument panels which feature a Projected Map Display (PMD) or the modernised configuration with a 6in x 8in Multi-Purpose Colour Display (MPCD).
